About the Collection

About the Collection

Our collection starts with the first shoe design, which will combine randomized colors of the
shoe’s outsole and upper design. The combination of these parts will create over 100 unique variations of this first design, creating an exclusive and well-sought-after collection.

We’ve got great expertise behind our deliverables, hiring industry experts to make the transition from 2D designs to physical products as seamless as possible. Our lead designer has worked for some of the largest brands in the industry, so you’re in good hands!

Artistic excellence meets top-tier craftsmanship, and you get a chance to be a part of it.

ROAD MAP

  • Stage 1

    Lacing Up

    The first stage of our development involves developing a community. We aim to bring together the footwear fam, and develop a community of fashion enthusiasts and lovers of web3. We’re going to make huge steps towards changing the fashion industry for good, and offering great utility to our holders (and wearers).

    You’re able to get involved by joining our Discord community, or contributing to the conversation on our Twitter.

  • Stage 2

    Fresh Kicks

    As mint day rolls around, we plan to hit the ground running by investing in a comprehensive marketing campaign to get our message out there. We’ll be running competitions alongside everything, in order to give back to our dedicated community. When we’re ready, our collection of 10 000 NFTs will go live, and you’ll get a chance to be a part of something exciting - and look good whilst doing it. Holders will receive both a 2D and 3D version of their NFT… with even more to come.

    As a form of instant utility, we’ll be redesigning our NFTs and creating a physical version of our designs to be displayed on a dedicated website, with each respective holder getting a free pair. Only you, as the holder of the matching NFT, can own your shoe design - completely one-of-a-kind.

    Our physical products will be created by our top-quality manufacturing partner based in Italy, making our physical products just as good as our NFTs.

  • Stage 3

    The Other Foot

    Stage 3 will see the release of our second collection, which will introduce a completely new design, and a brand new opportunity for potential holders to get their hands on a revolutionary fashion item. We’ll also be releasing an exclusive holders-only collection designed to thank our early supporters!

    Moving forward, 10% of our total revenue will be given to charity, decided by the community to make a difference. The introduction of more styles will kickstart our movement into full brand status, which will be supported by NFT influencers and sneaker brands worldwide.

    Besides that, we will be integrating our sneakers with all the metaverses, so you can be stylish both IRL and in web3.

  • Stage 4

    The Future

    The next stage of our project will include marketing and networking to ensure that our brand stays at the very top of the virtual fashion hierarchy. We’ll list our products on popular marketplaces, and keep producing new designs

    We’ll always give our holders a chance to have their say on what we do - we’re always open to hearing valuable feedback and ideas. Have your say in our Discord.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an NFT?

    It stands for Non-Fungible Token, and is a way of offering exclusive ownership, or virtual authenticity by minting transactions onto the blockchain, where they can be verified by anybody. It allows people to buy unique and one-of-a-kind tokens that give access to exciting benefits.

  • What is Metamask?

    Metamask is a crypto wallet that can be used to store your purchased NFTs. All you need to do is create an account, and connect your unique wallet to a menting website or marketplace, where you can purchase your NFTs.
